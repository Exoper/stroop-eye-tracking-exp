What is this study about?

This study aims to analyse the attention of students while they perform certain tasks online. 

What are the tasks?

You will be performing 3 simple tasks which tests your attention levels. You'll have clear instructions of what to do before each task. 

How much time will it take?

This should take you around 15 minutes. A progress bar is there on the top of the screen to indicate your progress in the trial.  Stay focused!
